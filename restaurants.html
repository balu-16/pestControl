Why It Matters

Pest Control is Critical for Food Businesses

A single pest sighting can destroy years of reputation. Our FSSAI-compliant treatments are scheduled around your business hours using food-safe products. We help hundreds of restaurants maintain pest-free environments that pass health inspections.

Common Restaurant Pest Challenges

  • Cockroaches in kitchen equipment and storage areas
  • Rodents attracted to food waste and storage
  • Drain flies and fruit flies in bar areas
  • Stored product pests in dry goods inventory
  • Ants invading food preparation surfaces
  • Flying insects in dining areas
