Understanding the Threat

The Silent Destroyers: Why Termite Control is Critical

Termites cause billions in property damage annually in India. These silent invaders consume wood 24/7, often undetected until significant damage occurs. Professional treatment and regular inspections are crucial for protecting your property.

Signs of Termite Infestation

  • Mud tubes on walls, foundations, or wooden structures
  • Hollow-sounding wood when tapped
  • Discarded wings near windows or doors (swarmer season)
  • Bubbling or peeling paint resembling water damage
  • Frass (termite droppings) resembling sawdust
  • Tight-fitting doors or windows due to wood warping

Comprehensive Termite Treatment Options

Our Process

Professional Termite Treatment Procedure

Our systematic approach ensures complete termite elimination and long-term protection.

01

Comprehensive Survey

Thorough inspection of foundation, wooden structures, and potential entry points using specialized detection equipment.

02

Treatment Planning

Based on our survey findings, we develop a customized treatment plan tailored to your property's specific needs. We consider factors such as construction type, soil conditions, infestation severity, and your budget to recommend the most effective solution.

03

Drill-Fill-Seal Treatment

For post-construction treatment, we drill 12mm holes at 1-foot intervals along the wall-floor junction, inject termiticide under pressure to ensure deep soil penetration, and seal the holes aesthetically. This creates a continuous chemical barrier around your property.

04

Warranty & Maintenance

We provide comprehensive warranty coverage with our treatments. Our Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC) includes periodic inspections and touch-up treatments to ensure continued protection. We're committed to keeping your property termite-free.
