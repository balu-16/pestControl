Understanding the Problem

Why Bed Bugs Are a Serious Concern

Bed bugs are parasitic insects that enter homes through luggage, furniture, or clothing. They hide in mattresses and furniture during the day, emerging at night to feed, leaving itchy welts. Their resistance to DIY treatments makes professional intervention essential.

Signs of Bed Bug Infestation

  • Itchy, red bite marks in a line or cluster pattern
  • Small blood stains on sheets or pillowcases
  • Dark spots (bed bug excrement) on mattresses or walls
  • Musty odor from bed bug scent glands
  • Shed skins or egg shells in hiding spots
  • Live bugs in mattress seams or furniture joints
