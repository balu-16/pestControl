Understanding the Problem

Cockroaches: More Than Just a Nuisance

Cockroaches carry disease-causing pathogens and contaminate food and surfaces. They reproduce rapidly – a single female can produce 30,000+ offspring yearly. Quick professional intervention prevents small problems from becoming major infestations.

Signs of Cockroach Infestation

  • Live cockroaches seen during day (indicates severe infestation)
  • Cockroach droppings resembling black pepper or coffee grounds
  • Egg cases (oothecae) in hidden areas
  • Musty, unpleasant odor in enclosed spaces
  • Smear marks along walls and surfaces
  • Damage to food packaging and organic materials

Areas We Treat for Cockroaches

Our Process

How Our Cockroach Treatment Works

A systematic approach for complete cockroach elimination.

01

Thorough Inspection

Our technicians inspect your entire property to identify cockroach species, locate harborage areas, assess infestation severity, and identify conditions conducive to cockroach activity. This information guides our treatment approach.

02

Targeted Treatment

We apply gel bait in strategic locations where cockroaches travel and hide. The treatment is placed in cracks, crevices, under appliances, inside cabinets, and other harborage areas. No preparation or vacating is required.

03

Monitoring Period

You'll notice significant reduction in cockroach activity within 7-10 days. The gel continues working for several weeks, eliminating cockroaches that come into contact with it or consume poisoned insects.

04

Follow-up Service

We schedule a follow-up visit to assess results, refresh bait applications as needed, and address any remaining concerns. For AMC customers, we provide regular quarterly treatments for year-round protection.
