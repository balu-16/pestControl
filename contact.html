FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you respond to my request?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 2-4 hours during business hours. For urgent pest situations, we offer same-day or next-day service in most areas.

Is the inspection really free?

Yes, our initial inspection and consultation are completely free with no obligation. We'll assess your situation and provide a detailed quote before any work begins.

Are your treatments safe for children and pets?

Absolutely. We use CIB-approved products that are safe for your family when applied according to guidelines. We also offer herbal and eco-friendly options.

Do you offer Annual Maintenance Contracts?

Yes, we offer flexible AMC plans for both residential and commercial properties. AMC customers enjoy priority service, regular visits, and discounted rates.
