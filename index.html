How It Works

Our 4-Step Hygiene Process

We follow a systematic, science-backed approach to pest control that ensures complete elimination and long-term prevention. Our process is designed to be thorough yet minimally disruptive to your daily routine.

Inspection

Our certified technicians conduct a comprehensive audit of your entire premises, examining potential entry points, nesting areas, and environmental factors that attract pests. This detailed assessment forms the foundation of your customized treatment plan.

Identification

Accurate pest identification is crucial for effective treatment. Our experts identify the exact species, assess the severity of infestation, and understand behavioral patterns to select the most appropriate and efficient treatment methodology.

Treatment

Using state-of-the-art equipment and eco-friendly, CIB-approved chemicals, our trained professionals apply targeted treatments. We use integrated pest management techniques that are safe for your family while being lethal to pests.

Prevention

Post-treatment, we provide detailed guidance on preventive measures and schedule follow-up visits to ensure complete eradication. Our AMC plans offer regular maintenance to keep your property pest-free year-round.
